About

Hi!

I am Bùi Quang Cường, originally come from Vietnam. People often refer to me by the given name Cường (/kɯɤŋᴬ²/ ). I was born and brought up in Anh Sơn—Miền Tây Xứ Nghệ (The Middle).

I am currently a Ph.D. student in the Institute of Software Security at TU Hamburg under the supervision of Prof. Riccardo Scandariato. My research topic is about automated repair of software vulnerabilities. Before, I obtained my MSc. degree in Computer Science and my BSc. degree in Information Technology, both at VNU – UET. In my spare time, I spontaneously enjoy writing blog posts to record my life and share my knowledge.
