About me

I am Bùi Quang Cường (IPA pronunciation: /ɓujᴬ² kwaːŋᴬ¹ kɯɤŋᴬ²/ ), originally come from Vietnam. People often refer to me by the given name Cường. I was born and brought up in Anh Sơn—Miền Tây Xứ Nghệ (The Middle).

Currently, I am a Ph.D. student in the Institute of Software Security at TU Hamburg under the supervision of Prof. Riccardo Scandariato. My research topic is about detecting and fixing software vulnerabilities. Before, I obtained my MSc. degree in Computer Science and my BSc. degree in Information Technology, both at VNU – UET.
